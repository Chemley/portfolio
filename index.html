About Me:

I am a writer, developer and front-end designer who excels at creating technical instructions and standard operating procedures in a deadline-driven environment. As an instructional designer, I worked with subject-matter experts, business leaders, and software developers to gather information, identify relevant data, and specify the target audience. The elearning courses, exams, quizzes, handouts, and emails I developed, were built using Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Captivate and designed for the web, which sparked my interest in front-end development.

The desire to learn front-end development led me to the Facebook Front-End Bootcamp at Grand Circus where I honed my skills in web development, coding, and responsive, mobile-first design.

I am looking to begin my career in software development in a role takes advantage of my background in design and passion for HTML, CSS, and mobile first design.
